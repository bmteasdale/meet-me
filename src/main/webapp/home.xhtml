Scheduling meetings made easy!


MeetMe is an online scheduling system that helps users schedule meetings without the hassle of back-and-forth emails.





Features


Efficient
Instantly find times that work for both organizers and attendees.
Email Reminders
All attendees are sent email reminders prior to a meeting.
Expandable
Works great for individuals and teams.
Flexible
Users can add unlimited attendees to a meeting.
Privacy
Users have the option to make their meetings public or private.
Timezone Management
MeetMe is able to seamlessly handle changes in timezones.



How It Works


1. Enter Meeting Details
Enter the names of the participants as well as the date in which the meeting will be set.
2. Select Meeting Time
Given the date, MeetMe will prompt you with a list of available time blocks for that day.
3. Invite Attendees
Once the meeting time has been selected, all attendees will be sent an email invitation. Attendees will also receive an email reminder prior to the meeting.
